ABOUT US

Modern Pie Company was set up with these three values in mind: to be crafted, healthy and balanced. We want our customers to be able to taste that our pies are not mass produced, are healthy, natural and wholesome. We create a daily menu which depends on season and locally sourced produce. This ensures that we are helping the smaller local businesses and bringing income to the community.
