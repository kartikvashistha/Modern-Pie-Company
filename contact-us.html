FAQ

  • What makes your pies special?

    • We bake our pies to order so they are always fresh. We have a secret recipe which makes our crusts unique!

  • How much does the delivery cost?

    • Our standard delivery charge is £2.99 or £5.99 for specific times.

  • How long does it usually take for delivery?

    • We bake to order so the pies will be fresh. Please check your order schedule to find out when it will be delivered.

  • Can I get morning delivery?

    • The service is charged at £5.99.

  • How long can my order stay in the box for?

    • The products can stay in the box for up to 48 hours.

  • Which of your pies are vegan

    • The cherry pie is vegan.

  • Which of your pies are vegetarian?

    • The vegetable quiche and all sweet pies are vegetarian. Please note, these may include egg.

  • Can I order a pie to be gluten free?

    • You can order a pie to be gluten free. Please note that we need 48 hours notice.
